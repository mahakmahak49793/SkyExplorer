Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Welcome to our FAQ page! Whether you're planning your next adventure or need details about our services, this is the place to find quick and reliable answers. We’ve compiled responses to the most commonly asked questions to save you time and make your experience with Sky Explorer as smooth as possible.

Explore topics like Bookings, Baggage Policies, Refunds, and more by scrolling through our carefully curated list of questions and answers. Each section is designed to address your concerns and provide the information you need for a smooth travel experience.

Let's make your journey with us hassle-free, from the moment you start planning to the time you land at your destination.

Happy exploring!

Bookings

How do I book a flight?
Visit our "Bookings" section, enter your travel details, and confirm your ticket.
Can I modify my booking?
Yes, visit the "Manage Bookings" section to make changes.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets for a seamless payment experience.
How do I receive my ticket after booking?
Your e-ticket will be sent to your registered email immediately after the payment is confirmed.
Can I book tickets for someone else?
Absolutely! Just enter the traveler’s details while booking, and the ticket will be issued in their name.

Baggage

What is the baggage allowance?
Economy passengers can carry 15kg of checked and 7kg of cabin baggage.
Can I purchase extra baggage?
Yes, additional baggage can be purchased during booking or later.
Are there any prohibited items in cabin baggage?
Yes, items like sharp objects, liquids over 100ml, and flammable substances are not allowed in cabin baggage.
What happens if my baggage exceeds the allowed weight?
Excess baggage fees will apply, calculated based on the weight and route of travel.
How do I report lost or damaged baggage?
Visit the airline counter at the airport or contact our customer support team immediately to file a report.

Refunds & Cancellations

How do I cancel my flight?
Log into your account and cancel via "Manage Bookings."
When will I receive my refund?
Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days.
Are all tickets refundable?
Refund policies depend on the ticket type. Non-refundable tickets may only be eligible for partial refunds.
Can I reschedule my flight instead of canceling it?
Yes, most tickets allow rescheduling. Visit the "Manage Bookings" section to check eligibility and make changes.
What happens if my flight is canceled by the airline?
In case of cancellations by the airline, you are entitled to a full refund or free rescheduling.

Travel Policies

Do I need a passport for domestic flights?
No, a government-issued ID is sufficient.
Are pets allowed on flights?
Yes, pets are allowed in the cabin or cargo with additional fees.
What is the check-in process?
You can check in online or at the airport counter. Online check-in opens 48 hours before departure.
What health documents are required for international travel?
You may need a COVID-19 vaccination certificate, negative test results, or other health documents based on the destination.
Are unaccompanied minors allowed to travel?
Yes, we offer special services for unaccompanied minors to ensure their safety during the journey.
